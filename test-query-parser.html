查询解析器测试

测试OpenSearch查询到Doris SQL的转换逻辑，特别是括号内逻辑连接符的处理。

测试用例：

测试用例 1: 括号内OR逻辑（msg字段）

msg:("walk in trackPageEventWhenFirstLoad" OR "walk in handleRouteChangeComplete")
期望输出: msg REGEXP "walk in (trackPageEventWhenFirstLoad|handleRouteChangeComplete)"

测试用例 2: 括号内OR逻辑（message字段）

message:("error occurred" OR "exception thrown")
期望输出: message REGEXP "error occurred|exception thrown"

测试用例 3: 复杂AND+OR组合

project_name:"test" AND msg:("error" OR "warning") AND level:ERROR
期望输出: project_name = "test" AND msg REGEXP "error|warning" AND level = "ERROR"

测试用例 4: 包含正则表达式的OR

msg:("test*" OR "debug*")
期望输出: (msg MATCH_PHRASE("test*") OR msg MATCH_PHRASE("debug*"))

测试用例 5: 嵌套括号处理

msg:("start" AND ("error" OR "warning"))
期望输出: msg MATCH_PHRASE("start") AND (msg MATCH_PHRASE("error") OR msg MATCH_PHRASE("warning"))
